Our Server

We are a small community of friends who enjoy playing Minecraft. Our server has been online since November the 10th 2010, however, we perform world resets on a regular basis (mostly just affecting our survival world). We have a few rules to make playing together more fun.

This includes:

  • Don't intentionally ruin another user's fun with e.q. griefing, non consensual PVP or overloading the server.
  • Follow rule 1 :)

If you have further questions please feel free to contact us.

All of our previous worlds are available for download sorted by date from oldest to newest. In addition to that, they also have a label appended to the name, which describes if the world was a survival world or creative world.
