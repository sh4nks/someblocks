About SomeBlocks

SomeBlocks is the central hub of our Team. In the past, this was just the domain for our Minecraft server, but because we liked this domain so much, we retired our old domain and made this the new one.

This place is mostly used to gather all the information about our projects and services we have. Most of our projects are Open Source and generally we believe that, the world would be a better place if there is more Open Source Software. We are also running our own Git service Gogs in case one needs a private repository (usually just university stuff).

Besides that, we also like to game from time to time and thats why we are also running a few game servers such as Minecraft (more to come) at gaming.someblocks.com.
